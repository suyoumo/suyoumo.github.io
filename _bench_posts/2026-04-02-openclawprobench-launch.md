OpenClawProBench is designed to evaluate model intelligence under OpenClaw instead of only measuring raw knowledge.

The benchmark emphasizes three-run stability, task-level diversity, and practical agent behavior: planning, tool use, constraint handling, recovery from errors, synthesis, and safety.

This bench blog will track methodology updates, new model results, and future benchmark extensions.
