A single successful run is often not enough to judge whether an agent is dependable.

OpenClawProBench highlights Pass^3 because repeated success is a stronger signal of usable intelligence under OpenClaw workflows. If a model only succeeds once by luck, the benchmark should expose that instability.

This makes the leaderboard more conservative, but also more informative.
